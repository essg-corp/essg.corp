What We
Do?
ESS G. Corporation manufactures the full spectrum of garment accessories—everything from bundle-cut paper to buttons and interlinings—right here in Bangladesh, eliminating what was once 100% import dependency by local factories. By producing domestically, we remove customs hurdles, slash lead times, keep costs low, and ensure that money stays within the country.
